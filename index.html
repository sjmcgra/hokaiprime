The Series

Welcome to The Hokai

THE HOKAI is a science fiction series that blends Generative AI with traditional animation and post production Set in the midst of an interstellar war, it follows characters whose lives intersect at the fault lines of survival, power, and transformation.

Produced as a one-person operation, the series blends traditional filmmaking discipline with cutting-edge AI generation technology to create something genuinely new. Generated by AI but crafted by human hands — a new form of cinema that embraces the aesthetics and possibilities of the medium.

This is not a demo. This is cinema — made in a way cinema has never been made before.
