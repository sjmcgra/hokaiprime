Kyrie

Protagonist — Episode One

A child navigating territories no child should enter. Kyrie moves through the D'ovra Insidia hunting grounds with a combination of instinct and inherited knowledge. The world she lives in does not accommodate survival — she does that herself.

Protagonist Survivor Episode 01

Admiral Horus

Command — The Fleet

A Maine Coon of uncommon authority, distinguished by the eye patch he earned in a conflict the records do not fully capture. Among the most visually consistent figures in the Hokai-verse — and among the most dangerous.

Commander Strategist Eye Patch

Fang

Warrior — Unknown Affiliation

Lion-like in form, encased in dark matte armor. Fang does not explain himself. His allegiances are circumstantial. His combat record is not. He exists at the edge of the Hokai-verse's moral framework, which is already a long way from center.

Warrior Dark Armor Unaligned
